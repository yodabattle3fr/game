Bounce Brawl

Local 2–4 player arena party game. Collect coins, steal points, and use powerups!
Players
2
Score limit
12
Controls per player (keyboard):
Player 1: WASD   •   Player 2: Arrow keys   •   Player 3: IJKL   •   Player 4: TFGH
Rules
  • Collect coins (+1).
  • Bumping a player briefly steals 1 coin (if they have one).
  • Powerups spawn: speed, shield, magnet.
  • First to score limit wins — or highest after timer ends.
Owner Panel
Tip: Play with friends and chaos! You can also hold a controller per player if you edit keys in the code.
